Welcome to the Red Blood Society

Red Blood Society is a Non-Government Organisation that was founded by Mr Ashok Mittal in the year 2005 and got registered in 2006 by the name of Red Blood Society. The Society now, has more than 3000 volunteers, who are very supportive of the cause for which the Society is working. The Society aims at providing Single Donor Platelets in emergency cases apart from providing blood and blood cells.
